What is livesociya?

livesociya is a social platform for real life. Instead of posting for attention, you post what you're actually doing — and people around you can join.

How it works

Post what you're doing

Studying in the library? Walking after class? Sitting in the canteen? Just post it.

People join

Others nearby see it and can join without awkward DMs or pressure.

Do it together

You meet, talk, work, walk, or just be around each other. That’s it.

Why we’re building this

College is full of people — but starting conversations is hard. livesociya makes it easy to just show up and be part of something.
